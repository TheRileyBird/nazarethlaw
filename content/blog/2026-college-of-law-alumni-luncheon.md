## Celebrating Excellence and Community

The 2026 College of Law Alumni Luncheon was a day of joy and camaraderie as we recognized this year's St. Ives Awardee, the Honorable Kern A. Reese, J.D. '77, while celebrating the achievements of the College of Law and our amazing community.

![2026 College of Law Alumni Luncheon](/src/assets/images/2026lunch.jpeg)

## Honoring the Honorable Kern A. Reese

This year's St. Ives Award was presented to the Honorable Kern A. Reese, J.D. '77, in recognition of his distinguished career and outstanding contributions to the legal profession. The St. Ives Award is one of the highest honors bestowed by the College of Law, celebrating alumni who exemplify excellence, integrity, and service to the community.

Judge Reese's distinguished career has been marked by:

- Decades of dedicated service to the judiciary
- Commitment to justice and the rule of law
- Mentorship of countless young attorneys
- Leadership in the legal community

His recognition at this year's luncheon reflects the deep respect he has earned from colleagues, students, and the broader legal community throughout his remarkable career.

## Recognizing Leadership and Service

A special thank you to the Honorable June Darensburg, J.D. '94, for her exemplary term as Law Alumni Board President. Her leadership and dedication have strengthened the bonds within our alumni community and advanced the mission of the College of Law.

Judge Darensburg's tenure as Board President has been characterized by:

- Strategic vision for alumni engagement
- Support for current law students
- Fostering connections across graduating classes
- Advancing the College of Law's reputation and standing

Her service has made a lasting impact on the alumni association and the law school community.

## The St. Ives Award Legacy

The St. Ives Award represents the highest recognition the College of Law bestows upon its alumni. Named after St. Ives of Kermartin, the patron saint of lawyers, the award honors alumni who have demonstrated:

- Outstanding professional achievement
- Dedication to the legal profession
- Service to the community
- Commitment to justice and ethical practice
- Leadership in their field

Judge Reese joins a distinguished list of past recipients who have made significant contributions to the legal profession and their communities.
